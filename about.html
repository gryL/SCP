About Us

SCP FOUNDATION — PUBLIC INFORMATION OFFICE
(Unclassified Overview — Revision ██)

The SCP Foundation is an independent, multinational organization responsible for the oversight of specialized containment and research initiatives conducted in cooperation with recognized governmental and scientific bodies. Our mandate is to ensure that sensitive materials and information remain secure, controlled, and properly managed in accordance with established regulatory frameworks.

The Foundation maintains a network of administrative and research facilities. Specific locations, personnel assignments, and operational details are restricted and are not included in this summary.

Our mission is guided by three core principles:

Secure
Maintain the integrity and confidentiality of Foundation assets.
Contain
Ensure that sensitive materials remain under controlled conditions.
Protect
Support global stability by preventing unauthorized access to restricted information.

The Foundation adheres to applicable international standards and cooperates with authorized agencies when appropriate. Due to the sensitive nature of our work, certain information may be withheld, redacted, or otherwise limited.
